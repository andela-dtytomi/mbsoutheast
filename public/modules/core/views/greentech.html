Green Technology

Simplifying Less Favorable Soil Conditions

Post Tension can be used on many types of soils and conditions.  In instances where soil conditions are not ideal, using Post Tension can save money by avoiding costly excavation and pad preparations..

Less Material Translates to Lower Costs

Post Tension design typically uses much less concrete and steel than conventional applications.  This direct correlates to less overall costs.

Contributes to LEED points and Green Building Certification

The U.S. Green Building Council’s rating system gives builders LEED points towards a Green Building Certification if Post Tension is used.
